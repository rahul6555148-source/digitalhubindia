🚀 Getting Started - Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Choose Your Tool (Day 1)

Select ONE AI tool that aligns with your skills. If you like writing, start with ChatGPT. If you're creative, try Midjourney or Canva.

Step 2: Learn the Basics (Days 2-7)

Watch YouTube tutorials, read documentation, and practice creating outputs. Spend at least 2 hours daily learning.

Step 3: Build a Portfolio (Week 2)

Create 5-10 sample works to showcase your abilities. This will be crucial for attracting clients.

Step 4: Create Profiles on Freelance Platforms (Week 3)

Sign up on Upwork, Fiverr, Freelancer.in, and LinkedIn. Optimize your profile with keywords related to your AI service.

Step 5: Start Bidding/Applying (Week 4)

Apply to 10+ jobs daily. Initially, accept lower-paying projects to build reviews and ratings.

Step 6: Scale Up (Month 2+)

As you get reviews, increase your rates. Aim for ₹500-1000 per hour by month 3.
